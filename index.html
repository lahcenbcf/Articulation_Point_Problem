Interactive Graph Creator

Current Mode: None

Instructions:

1. Click "Add Nodes Mode" and click in the canvas to add nodes

2. Click "Add Edges Mode" and drag between nodes to create edges

3. Select nodes/edges and click "Delete Selected" to remove them

4. Click "Find Articulation Points" to highlight critical nodes in red

5. Click "Reset Colors" to remove highlighting

6. Click "Clear Graph" to start over
